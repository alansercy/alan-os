# AgentOS — Product Spec

## What It Is
AI automation stack for real estate agents. Reduces manual workload across inbox management, content generation, lead follow-up, and CRM updates.

## Proof of Concept
**Client:** Loretta Sercy — lorettasercy.com
- Inbox reduced from 27,500 → 3,400 emails (88% reduction)
- Full content engine built and running
- Live n8n workflows: 2.1, 2.2, 2.4, 2.5, 2.6, C
