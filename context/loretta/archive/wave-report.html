What This Is

Not a market update.
An interpretation.

The Wave Report is a monthly editorial on East Dallas growth patterns — the corridor of communities running from Royse City and Fate through Caddo Mills, Greenville, Farmersville, and Blue Ridge that is in the middle of a meaningful, complicated shift.

Most market reports tell you what sold and for how much. This one tells you what's changing and what it means — the infrastructure gaps, the acreage fragmentation, the second-order effects that don't show up in a comp sheet but will absolutely show up in your daily life three years from now.

Published by Loretta Sercy — a realtor who lives in this corridor, works in it every day, and has a vested interest in people understanding it clearly before they commit to it.

What it is
Regional interpretation. Editorial observation. Context that should inform a decision but never replace one.
What it isn't
A data summary. A prediction. A market report. An argument for or against buying anywhere specific.
Who it's for
Buyers thinking about moving east. Sellers understanding what their market is becoming. Anyone trying to understand a place before committing to it.
