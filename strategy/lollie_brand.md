## The Vision

A children's book series that becomes a brand platform — books, merch, school visits, speaking, licensing, and eventually a publishing imprint focused on neurodiversity. The emotional authority comes from lived experience, not clinical expertise. That is the differentiator.

**Long-term exit:** Licensing deal with major children's publisher or acquisition by educational media company.
